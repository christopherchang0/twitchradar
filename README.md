A dashboard that analyzes twitch performances, giving authentic userfeedback based off their metrics.

Purpose: Streamers on Twitch may lack a competent understanding of their own rankings compared to other streamers not only overall, but in terms of niches, frequency, etc. Incremental provides just that for streamers, streamlining efficient metrics for streamers to vague their own live performances.

## Twitch API:
    - https://dev.twitch.tv/docs/api/


## Tech Stack:
    - Frontend: React, Tailwind, Tableau (embedded)
    - Backend: Python, Supabase, FirebaseMatplotlib, Seaborn, Airtable
